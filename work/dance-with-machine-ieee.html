DANCE WITH MACHINE

Performance Dates: June 21, 2019

Event: IEEE GEM 2019

Performance Developers and Designers: Tianshu Zhao, Chase Shimmin, Douglas Duhaime

Dancer & Choreographer: Raymond Pinto

The performance is based upon and for the demonstration of Beyond Imitation: Generative and Variational Choreography via Machine Learning research project.


Experimentation for the performance -- The sound is generated and controlled by the movement which is tracked by the vicon mocap system.

BIOGRAPHY


Tianshu is a visual experience designer and a director of live performances, who founded Triangle Collective, a design lab in 2016. She explores projects at the intersection of spatial design, multimedia storytelling, and technology to create time-based experiences.

Her recent projects include visual design for Flying Tigers Flying Away on Off-Broadway, direction and multimedia design for Lost and Found on Off-Off Broadway, and visual experience design for A Well 2.0 in Chicago Rhino Festival and Quantum Voyages with Theatre Department and Physics Department at the University of Illinois. She has worked internationally on a number of interdisciplinary projects with organizations, including Yale Tsai CITY, Harvard XMedia Lab at GSE, North America Maple Culture Center, Whitney International Project, and PEERChina. She is adept at Adobe Creative Suite, multiple programming languages, and live video production tools.
